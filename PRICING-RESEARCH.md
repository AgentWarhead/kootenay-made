# Canadian Web Design Agency Pricing Research
*Compiled: March 30, 2026 | All prices in CAD unless noted*

---

## TL;DR — Where the Market Sits

| Tier | Price Range | Who It's For |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| DIY (Wix/Squarespace/Shopify) | $20–$100/mo | Hobbyist / side hustle |
| Budget freelancer | $500–$2,000 one-time | Rock-bottom, template-only |
| Mid-tier freelancer | $2,000–$6,000 | Most small businesses |
| Small/mid agency | $3,000–$15,000 | Growing businesses, more features |
| Full-service agency | $15,000–$75,000+ | Enterprise, custom builds |

**Industry sweet spot for a "real" small business website: $3,000–$8,000 CAD**

## 8. PREMIUM vs. BUDGET AGENCY COMPARISON

### Budget/Template Agencies
- Price: $500–$3,000 one-time OR $29–$79/month subscription
- Examples: Direct Web Design ($29–$79/mo), Intr-Ango (Toronto, $650–$1,250)
- What you get: Template-based, limited customization, minimal strategy

### Mid-Market Agencies
- Price: $3,000–$10,000 one-time
- Examples: Clio Websites (Calgary, ~$3,000 avg), Stigan Media (BC, $2,000–$4,500)

### Premium/Full-Service Agencies
- Price: $15,000–$75,000+
- Examples: Setsail Marketing (Vancouver), Idea Marketing (Vancouver, $7,000–$30,000+)

## 9. KEY OBSERVATIONS FOR POSITIONING

1. **kootenay.design is mid-market priced** ($1,000/1-page, $3,500/5-page) — right in the typical range, maybe slightly below mid-market for quality work

2. **No local Kootenay competitor has public pricing** (ProCreative, Ello Design, Tenai, i9design, Anthony Maley all require contact). This means kootenay.design has a transparency advantage.

3. **The AI services gap is real** — nobody in Canada offers a packaged "AI business setup" service at accessible small business prices. Closest thing is enterprise-priced AI integration ($15K–$65K). There's clear white space at the $500–$2,000 level for "get your business set up with AI tools."

4. **Maintenance retainers** at $150–$300/month are the market norm. Anyone charging less ($29–$79) is the discount tier. Premium maintenance is $300–$500/month.

5. **Shopify e-commerce** is its own category — local businesses wanting online stores expect to pay $3,000–$10,000 for a proper build. DIY Shopify is an option but requires support.

6. **Vancouver price premium** is real — agencies there charge 20–40% more than smaller BC cities.
